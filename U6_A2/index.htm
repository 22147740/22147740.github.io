Celtec is a small independent games company developing original titles for desktop and mobile platforms. Celtec combines both talent and passion to create games that educate and entertain children and adults alike. Celtec is located in North East Wales, UK and have developed games for mobile (iOS, Android), desktop and consoles (Playstation, XBox, Nintendo Switch). Ask anyone at Celtec what the secret is and they will tell you. It's about creativity, vision, the Tech and the sense of belonging at Celtec. We reached where we are today by raising the game, empowering the people and raising the passion to make the greatest cross platform games in the country!

At Celtec, we utilize the latest game development technology to ensure that our games reach gamers all around the world. Whether they play on mobile devices, computers, or the latest console technology, we strive to provide the smooth gaming and rich gaming world that gamers expect. As an independent game development company, we are influenced by our community and our relationships with the education sector and the wider creative industry. This helps shape our ongoing journey as we strive to push the boundaries and try out new ideas and move onto more platforms as we seek to create a creative future fueled by passion and imagination.

Looking ahead, Celtec remains committed to growth as a creative studio while respecting our origins as an independent entity. We strive to make games that challenge ideas, foster learning, and bring people together around common experiences. By embracing new technologies, supporting new talent, and continually refining our craft, we hope to make games that make a lasting impact on players and the world. Our vision is quite simple: make important games that inspire, engage, and spark imagination for years to come.
